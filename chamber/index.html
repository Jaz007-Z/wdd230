Purpose

the goal of the chamber of commerce for guilds to promote legislation that will ensure the strict quality that comes from the tradition of organized guild trade. the guilds have served as a way of ensuring quality goods will be delivered to the community. The system of apprenticeship, journeymen, and guild masters has ensured top notch and goods and quality education into the different crafts. We promote corporation amongst guilds and the futher sucess of their members.
