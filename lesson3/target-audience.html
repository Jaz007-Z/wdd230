Target Audience

Guild Masters

Guild masters are masters of their craft who use their expertise to lead the crafts to success through quality products. They have all presented a masterwork as proof of their qualifications to be able to ensure their absolute knowledge and expertise in a craft that will allow to them lead their craft forward to new heights as an owners of a workshop. We give them further resources and information to help them colaborate and combine their talents.

Journeymen

Journeymen are proven members of their craft having undergone a exaustive education as an apprentice. They may seeks to become a master and to have build up the guild and its crafts expertise and success. We offer resources for journeymen to be able to collorabte on ideas and to see how they can help further the craft overall.

  • Scenario 1: A main page to inform show what the comber of commerce has to offer at a glance along with urges to join and any special bussiness events that deserve a spotlight.
  • Scenario 2:A page to give information about the populace and what would provide good knowledge for a business to have about local evnets and prefernces.
  • Scenario 3: A page of all local guilds and their respective workshops in the area.
  • Scenario 4: A page about joiing the chamber of commerce and the added benefits of further guild collaboration. Application forms and different membership ranks and perks that come from different guild positions will also be listed.
  • Scenario 5: Contact information for the chamber of commerce along with guild masters involved with leading the chamber of commerce. Contact forms and requests to get involved/learn about joining a guild or the chamber of commerce will also be given.
